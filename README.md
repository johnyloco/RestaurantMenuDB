🍷 Gastronomy Menu & Allergy Safety App
Developed by Aleks Popov

Combining 10+ years of 5-star hospitality experience on the Swiss Riviera with modern software development to create a safer, more intuitive dining experience.

🚀 The Vision
In the world of luxury hospitality, guest experience and health are paramount. This project was born from a passion for food and a commitment to guest safety. The core focus is a robust Allergy Management System that allows guests to filter menus based on their health requirements, ensuring a safe and seamless 5-star experience.
