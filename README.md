🍷 # Gastronomy Menu & Allergy Safety App #


👨‍💻 ## About the Author ##

Hi, my name is Aleks Popov.
My career is driven by two great passions:

 **App Development and Hospitality.**


With more than 10 years of experience in high-end hospitality and guest experience, I have identified specific areas where technology can improve the service industry.

By combining my two passions, I decided to build a solution that bridges the gap between digital convenience and the art of hosting.


I developed this online menu specifically for the restaurant where I work on the Swiss Riviera. While there is always more to improve, my main priority with this application is food allergy safety.

Working in 5-star hotels has taught me that taking care of a guest means, above all, taking care of their health. This app is designed to ensure that every guest can dine with confidence, transparency, and safety.



🛠️ ##How to Use the App##


**1. The Home Page***
The landing page of the app features the restaurant where I work full-time and provides information about the beautiful area where I live on the Swiss Riviera.

**2. Initial Setup: Allergy Database**
To ensure the best experience, food allergies must be added via the admin panel for each restaurant. To make this easier for you, I have integrated the Allergy Management link directly into the Navigation Menu. Please add your specific allergens manually there first.

**5. Drinks & Wine Selection**
The app also allows for the management of drinks and wines. This is crucial because many cocktails contain hidden allergens like celery, eggs, or milk, and many wines contain sulfites. Every drink is tracked with the same level of health-conscious detail as our food.
